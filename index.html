SITE PLAN

Royal Villas is an exclusive gated community of exquisitely crafted villas and high-end amenities executed by Sankalp Developers. High-Quality development delivered in time is their motto. Lying in the lap of nature, this 29 acre expanse has been crafted into 277 exclusive plots is located at Shahapur near Mumbai. Royal Villas is a well developed Villa Project with Internal tar roads lined by mature trees making it an avenue complementing the natural beauty of the place. Beautiful Vaastu Complete independent Villas & Twin Villas to choose from which have private garden Covering more than 30% of the plot area. This comes with common amenities which include a Ready to use Club House which provides a Restaurant, Community Centre, Swimming Pool with Rain Dance, Indoor Games, Outdoor Gaming area, Children's Play Area & lawn and a fully furnished and air conditioner Guest Rooms are also available. A Royal Lifestyle awaits you at Royal Villas.
Sankalp Developers is promoted by partners Nilesh Raut & Ganesh Raut who are also brothers with more than 20 years of experience as Infrastructure Contractors where they have been involved in buildings, Roads, Bridges, Tunnels, Water & Power infrastructure. They have worked on various infrastructure projects commissioned by PWD, CPWD, BMC, and other local agencies.
